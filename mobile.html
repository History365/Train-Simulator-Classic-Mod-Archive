Who We Are

We are a dedicated community committed to preserving the legacy of Train Simulator mods. As the virtual landscape of Train Simulator evolves, many websites hosting invaluable mods are unfortunately facing the threat of closure, putting years of creativity and passion at risk of being lost forever.

Our mission is simple: To archive and safeguard these mods, ensuring that the rich history and diverse creations within the Train Simulator community remain accessible to enthusiasts, modders, and historians alike.

At the Train Simulator Mod Archive, we meticulously curate and categorize mods from various sources, ranging from locomotives and rolling stock to routes and scenery enhancements. Our archive is not just a repository; it's a testament to the creativity, dedication, and love for trains shared by individuals around the globe.
